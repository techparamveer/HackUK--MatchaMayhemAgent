# Matcha Mayhem mini hack — café venue shortlist

Cafés and café-style spaces that can hold roughly **20–30 people** and are
used to hosting community events, for the in-person Matcha Mayhem mini hack.

Compiled from public listings on 17 July 2026. **Capacities, prices and even
whether a venue still trades change often — verify everything with the venue
before promising anything.** Remember the audience is largely 16–18: daytime
slots, no licensed-premises-only spaces, and Wi-Fi + power sockets are
non-negotiable for a hack.

## Free matcha, anywhere

If the chosen venue doesn't serve matcha itself, a mobile matcha bar keeps the
promise on site:

- [Alchème Studio matcha bar](https://www.alchemestudio.com/pages/matcha-bar-at-your-event-in-london) — hand-prepared matcha bar for London events
